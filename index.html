Welcome

A living, breathing English classroom

Welcome! I'm a foreign English teacher at Nanhsing Elementary School, and I'm thrilled to share our journey toward becoming a truly global campus. This site is a living portfolio of our Bilingual Initiative — the vibrant ways our students meet English every single day.

Our mission is to move beyond the textbook. By weaving English into international partnerships, the creative arts, and daily school life, we give students authentic chances to listen, speak, read, and write — and to fall in love with learning.
