Our Mission

Confidence and curiosity, every day

Welcome! I'm a foreign English teacher at Nanhsing Elementary School, and I'm thrilled to share our journey toward becoming a truly global campus. This site is a living portfolio of our Bilingual Initiative — the vibrant ways our students meet English every single day.

By integrating English into various school contexts — from international partnerships to creative arts — we give our students authentic opportunities to develop their listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills. Explore our four specialized corners below.
